Fettucine Carbonara

This fettuccine carbonara dish is made by cooking bacon, shallots, onion, and garlic, then tossing it with fettuccine pasta and a creamy sauce.



Ingredients

This is one serving.

  • 5 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 shallots, diced
  • 1 pound bacon, cut into strips
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettucine pasta
  • 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 egg yolks
  • salt and pepper to taste
